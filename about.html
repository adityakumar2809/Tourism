ALL ABOUT TREKINDIA

Back in 2018 we set off on the open road with a van full of young explorers and one mission: To see the real India, and have fun along the way. Today our ethos hasn't changed. We'll show you India's big highlights from bustling cities to jaw-dropping National Parks, and incredible places so hidden only locals like us know about them (but we're happy to share) TrekIndia's distinctive style of travel offers something different to your ordinary guided tour or package holiday. Every year thousands of young people from all over the world find that our group adventure holidays offer a great value, easy and dependable and fun way to explore India.



ORIGINAL ADVENTURES SINCE '18

It's our Ist year in 2018, which makes us the longest running and most experienced small group operator in India. Knowing the places we visit so well, from the best beaches in Goa to our favourite music haunts in Manali and those awesome little stops along the way is a huge reason behind our success

INDIA'S SPECIALIST FOR 18-38s

Our tours are designed specifically for young, international travellers aged 18-38, so you're guaranteed to be travelling with people of a similar age. All with a sense of fun, adventure and a keen desire to explore - you couldn't meet a nicer crowd! So whether you're travelling alone or with friends, you'll soon be surrounded with like-minded people who will quickly become your new best trek mates
